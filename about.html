About

Lush · Optic

(/lʌʃ ˈɒptɪk/)

Adjective

1. Luxuriant and providing great sensory pleasure.

2. Relating to the eye or vision.

Lush Optic is a visual artist and live painter based out of Denver, Colorado. After a decade of sharpening his skills, he studied under leading artists in the fine art community for three years. He is known for his use of colorful geometric perspectives with interesting dimensions.

Lush Optic’s work has been displayed in galleries around the Denver Metropolitan area including Catalyst and Spectra Art Space. He frequently live paints at venues and events such as Cervantes Masterpiece Ballroom, Your Mom’s House Live Music Venue, and River Bar & Art Gallery.
